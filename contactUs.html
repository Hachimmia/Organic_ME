Where we are


Here we are, Kelso! In the beautiful Scottish Borders, surrounded by nature, artisans and farmers. There are a lot of activities that you can do here, such as fishing on the River Tweed, alpaca experience, clay pigeon shooting, archery, tomahawk throwing and many more. Outdoor fans and nature lovers will find a wide range of sports and leisure activities around them.
