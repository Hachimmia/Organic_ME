Shop

Welcome to our shop. Here you can find a vast assortment of local organic vegetables, artisan products for the home as well as for the person, delicious and special foods prepared in our café and sold in our shop, especially for people who cannot stay for a long time. Every product is organic local and plastic-free. In our shop, you can find a healthy alternative, for humans and the environment, to all the products that are in the supermarket.

Our Organic Honey

The way in which good honey is produced is very important and we, at Organic ME, are aware of it. Our honey respects the life of bees and therefore also the environment, as bees are very important for the latter. Our honey is authentic, we don't add any additives to it. We produce honey, that is 100% natural, free from GMO's and pesticides. Come and try it in our shop!
