Who we are

Organic ME is a growing family company started 5 years ago in the border region of Scotland. We started running the shop and the cafe, after that we added the gardening course because a lot of customers asked us where to start with it. We're proud of all we're doing for people and the environment.


Our values

We concern a lot about climate change, we respect the environment and we really try to become a Net Zero company. All our products are plastic-free and local, also made by local artisans. We recycle everything in our shop and we make compost to fertilize the cultivations.

Our products

"From land to plate" is our motto. All our products are genuine, organic, local and fresh every day. Also, we sell organic homeware and clothing products provided by local artisans as well as soap and candles which from the laboratory end up directly in the shop.


Our parters

Homethings and Good Club are two of our partners who fight like us to make the world a cleaner place with less pollution and less waste of materials, as well as providing super valid and very good products. Here, you can read more about Homethings and Good Club.
